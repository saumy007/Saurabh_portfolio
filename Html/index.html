About

Turning Policy
Into Practice

Saurabh is a seasoned government and public sector professional who has spent over 9 years working at the intersection of policy, business development, and digital inclusion — across Big 4 firms like KPMG and PwC, multilaterals like UNDP and IFAD, and central government programs.

His work focuses on one core challenge: helping small businesses and rural enterprises access markets, finance, and government support at scale. He does this by building partnerships — between state governments and e-commerce platforms, between small businesses and banks, between rural artisans and national brands.

Across his career, he has managed programs in Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh, Haryana, and Jharkhand — working with departments of Rural Development, MSME, and Commerce, and multilateral partners including the World Bank and IFAD.

He holds a Post Graduate Diploma in Development Studies from the Entrepreneurship Development Institute of India (EDII), Ahmedabad.
